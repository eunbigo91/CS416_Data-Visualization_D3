USA: Covid-19 Vaccinated Rate And New Cases

The U.S. was the first country that had 1 million confirmed cases. The U.S. also had about one-fifth of the world's confirmed cases and deaths. As the vaccinated rate increased, the daily new cases seemed to decrease. However since July, the new cases continued to increase, and according to CDC, theDelta variant accounted for 83% of all sequenced case [1]
