Israel: Covid-19 Vaccinated Rate And New Cases

Israel was prioritized for receiving the Pfizer vaccine. In exchange, Israel committed to send Pfizer medical data pertaining to the vaccinations, including side effects, efficacy, and length of time it takes to develop antibodies, for different age groups.[1] With this effort, new cases seemed to decrease at one point. But recently, new cases are rising and the Israel government is offering a 3rd round of doses for the vaccines.[2]
